Thank You

Although really the beginning of my journey, the making of this project could be delivered as a story on it's own. Trial, tribulation, triumph, exasperation, excitement, and pride mark my individual journey but I'd be remissed to fail to mention the many many many hands that held me up, challenged me, and/or pushed me along the way on my pursuit of my dream. I want to use this space to thank you and shed light on this story. Without all of you there is no me and I am incredibly thankful.

Firstly thank you to my Mother, Liza. I dedicate this album to you. This album uses the sun to tell the story of the cyclical nature of life and love. Thank you for being strong and brave enough to break your cycle and enable me to pursue great heights. The sun does in fact come tomorrow <3

Thank you to my family Matthew, Jillian, Charlie, Grace, Harrison, Grant, Lil Matt, Stella, and Sophia for constantly inspiring, loving, and motivating me to be great for all of you. Thank you to JJ and David for fostering the music in me from an early age and never telling me I couldn't. Thank you to my grandparents and all my aunts and uncles and cousins - there's way too many of you haha - for loving me and supporting me. I think of you all every day.

Thank you to all my friends but especially the friends that have shown themselves as family to me. Will, thank you for being my right hand and co-conspirator to all my worst ideas haha. Sam, thank you for your constant support and your never being afraid to tell me I'm wrong. Robin, thank you for unrelenting support in getting this thing done, on every level, this thing wouldn't be alive if it weren't for you. Ashton, thank you for your many late nights listening to the worst versions of this project and helping me iron things out. Declan, David, Jenna, Molly, and Brendan thank you for making college worth attending. Ali and Robert thanks for keeping in touch no matter the distance. I love you all forever.

Thank you to all my music homies, mentors, and collaborators. Thank you to Sam and Phil for taking a shot on an eager kid looking to learn anything and everything about music. Kez, Alex, Zeke, Kyle, Nick and Brendan thank you for answering every question, listening to my music, and showing me that it all was really possible. Mike, Lou, Kam, Marc, Nathan, Gabe and Frank thanks for your contagious love for what you do and the latest of nights at Jungle haha. Gina and Dean thank you for your friendship and memories I'll never forget. Making music with you both has been some of the happiest times of my life. Dean thank you for sharing your families' meatballs. That was real classy of you <3

Thank you to all that contributed creatively - whether it made the final cut or not - I'm grateful for your time. Thank you Robin, Harmony, Philip, Ishmail, Gbemi, Ragan, Alec, and Caroline. Thank you to everyone that came together to offer your ears on my pursuit of a great and lasting project. I learned something every time you listened and it genuinely helped shape what you hear today.

Thank you to all that have supported me along the way, believed in the story I've been trying to tell, and moved the needle on the music I've been trying to make my whole life. I pinch myself at how blessed I am by all of you. Thank you <3

Jake
